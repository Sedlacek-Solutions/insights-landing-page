Insights for Apps

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Insights for Apps (“Insights,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) provides a mobile and desktop client application for viewing App Store Connect analytics, transactions, customer reviews, and managing review responses on Apple platforms (iOS and macOS).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use Insights.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account & Authentication Information

When you sign in using Sign in with Apple, we process:

  • Apple Sign-In identity token
  • Apple subject identifier
  • Email address (if provided by Apple)
  • Optional user role

Authentication tokens processed by our backend include:

  • Access token (15-minute lifetime)
  • Refresh token (7-day lifetime)

1.2 App Store Connect Data

When you connect your App Store Connect account, we process business data retrieved from Apple’s App Store Connect API, including:

  • App metadata (app ID, SKU, title, bundle ID, locale, categories, privacy policy URL)
  • Analytics aggregates (downloads, sessions, purchases, crashes, device, platform, territory, source)
  • Transaction and sales records (SKU, proceeds, currency, territory, subscription events)
  • Customer reviews (title, body, rating, reviewer nickname, territory, date)
  • Developer review responses (body and state)

This data is used solely to provide Insights’ functionality.

1.3 GitHub Integration Data

If you connect GitHub, we process the information needed to provide optional issue discovery and issue-creation workflows, including:

  • GitHub account, organization, installation, and repository identifiers
  • Repository names and metadata for repositories you authorize or map to an app
  • GitHub access tokens, installation tokens, or authorization records needed to operate the integration
  • Open issue titles, descriptions, labels, states, and related issue metadata used for issue discovery
  • Issue titles, descriptions, labels, and review context you choose to create or send to GitHub

GitHub integration data is used only to provide the connected GitHub workflow.

1.4 Notifications

If you enable notifications:

  • Notification permission status
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) device token

Push notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time in system settings.

1.5 Operational & Log Data

Our backend collects limited operational data for security and reliability purposes:

  • Request metadata (HTTP method, path, status, latency)
  • Masked user identifiers
  • Partially redacted IP addresses
  • Metrics and counters

1.6 Purchase, Subscription & Credit Data

When you subscribe, purchase top-up credits, or use Intelligence Credits, we process information needed to verify and fulfill those transactions, including:

  • App Store product identifiers
  • Apple transaction identifiers and signed transaction payloads
  • Subscription period, expiration, revocation, and environment metadata
  • Credit balances, credit ledger entries, feature costs, and usage history

1.7 AI-Assisted Feature Content

When you use AI-assisted features, we process the content and context needed to provide those features, such as selected customer reviews, revenue and acquisition analytics context, prompts, filters, draft settings, translation language choices, generated drafts, translations, Review Chat messages, Revenue Chat messages, and Acquisition Chat messages, and GitHub issue-discovery context when you enable that integration.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and manage sessions
  • Retrieve and display App Store Connect data
  • Enable customer review management and responses
  • Connect review feedback to authorized GitHub repositories and create issue candidates or issues when requested
  • Provide analytics and reporting features
  • Provide AI-assisted chat, revenue and acquisition analysis, drafting, translation, and review-workflow features
  • Verify purchases, manage subscriptions, and maintain Intelligence Credits balances
  • Deliver optional push notifications
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and monitor system health
  • Improve performance and reliability

3. Local Storage on Your Device

Insights stores certain information locally:

Keychain (Secure Storage)

  • App Store Connect credentials
  • GitHub authorization records or tokens, if needed for a connected GitHub integration
  • Authentication tokens
  • User identifiers and roles

Keychain items may be synchronized using iCloud Keychain, depending on system settings.

UserDefaults

  • Selected app ID
  • Analytics filters and timeframes
  • Selected GitHub repository mappings and integration preferences
  • Onboarding and demo state
  • Navigation state

Local Files

  • Cached app summaries and analytics data stored under the app’s Application Support directory

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following parties when necessary:

  • Apple - Sign in with Apple, App Store Connect API, Apple Push Notification service (if enabled), and in-app purchase or subscription processing
  • GitHub - optional repository connection, issue discovery, and issue creation features that you authorize
  • AI Service Providers - AI-assisted chat, drafting, translation, and language-processing features
  • Infrastructure Providers - Cloud hosting, logging, and monitoring providers used to operate the service

AI service providers receive only the content and context needed to provide the requested AI-assisted feature. Do not submit information to AI-assisted features unless you have the right to process it through Insights and its providers.

We do not sell personal data or share it for advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

  • Authentication data is retained while your account is active.
  • You may disconnect App Store Connect at any time, which deletes stored credentials and cached data.
  • You may disconnect GitHub at any time, which removes stored GitHub authorization records and repository mappings where supported.
  • Purchase, subscription, and credit ledger records may be retained as needed for account history, fraud prevention, dispute handling, tax, accounting, legal, or platform compliance purposes.
  • AI-assisted request and response content may be retained only as needed to provide the feature, operate the service, troubleshoot issues, or maintain safety and abuse-prevention controls.
  • Logs and operational data are retained for a limited period for security and diagnostics.

Retention durations may evolve as the product matures.

6. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app.

When you delete your account:

  • Your authentication records and sessions are deleted from our backend
  • Local credentials, tokens, and cached data are cleared

Historical analytics, App Store Connect data, purchase records, credit ledger records, and operational records may be retained in aggregated or anonymized form, or where retention is needed for fraud prevention, dispute handling, tax, accounting, legal, or platform compliance.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including:

  • Secure Keychain storage
  • Encrypted network communication
  • Authentication and authorization checks
  • Rate limiting and request redaction
  • Backup and restore procedures

8. Children’s Privacy

Insights is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccuracies
  • Delete your account
  • Restrict or object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@sedlaceksolutions.com.

10. Legal Bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Where applicable, we process data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate interests (security, reliability, fraud prevention, abuse prevention)
  • User consent (notifications)

Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date.
