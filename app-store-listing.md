## Description

Insights for Apps is a native iPhone, iPad, and Mac workspace for developers and app teams who manage App Store growth, monetization, customer reviews, ratings, and responses.

Bring App Store growth, monetization, and trust workflows into one focused place. Use the sidebar to move between the operating areas that shape your app business without losing the app, account, and time range context around them.

Use Acquisition Chat to ask what changed across impressions, product page views, downloads, and conversion. Track which sources, territories, devices, and page types are turning App Store attention into downloads.

Use Revenue Chat to ask about proceeds, sales, purchases, paying users, and revenue mix. Track which territories, devices, source types, and segments are contributing the most revenue, with subscription health close to the monetization metrics it affects.

Use Review Chat and AI-assisted workflows to understand review patterns, draft contextual responses from the selected review, adjust tone and length, preview translations, and prepare Apple-facing removal requests when a review needs moderation. You stay in control of what gets sent.

Insights for Apps helps you:

- Chat across customer reviews, sentiment, complaints, and feature requests
- Create GitHub issue candidates from recurring review complaints and feature requests
- Ask Revenue Chat questions across proceeds, sales, purchases, paying users, and revenue mix
- Ask Acquisition Chat questions across impressions, product page views, downloads, and conversion
- Generate review responses with context from the selected review
- Translate international reviews and preview translated replies
- Filter reviews by app version, territory, rating, and response status
- Track rating patterns, review volume, revenue metrics, acquisition funnel metrics, and distribution
- Compare revenue by source, territory, device type, page type, and segment
- Compare acquisition by source, territory, device type, and page type
- Prepare concise removal-request drafts for reviews that need escalation
- Keep App Store Connect growth, monetization, and trust work organized in a native iPhone, iPad, and Mac workspace

Insights for Apps starts free, with optional Pro upgrades available in the app for more monthly intelligence credits.

App Store Connect credentials are stored securely in Keychain and used only to connect to App Store Connect. Insights for Apps uses Apple-provided APIs but is not affiliated with or endorsed by Apple.

Manage App Store growth, monetization, and trust from one focused workspace on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
