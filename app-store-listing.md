## Subtitle

App reviews and acquisition

## Promotional Text

## Description

Insights for App Store Connect is a Mac-first workspace for developers and app teams who manage App Store customer reviews, ratings, responses, and acquisition analytics.

Bring your review and acquisition workflow into one focused place. Ask Review Chat what users love, where complaints keep coming up, how sentiment is changing, and which product requests are showing up across your feedback.

Use Acquisition Chat to ask what changed across impressions, product page views, downloads, and conversion. Track which sources, territories, devices, and page types are turning App Store attention into downloads.

Use AI-assisted workflows to draft contextual responses from the selected review, adjust tone and length, preview translations, and prepare Apple-facing removal requests when a review needs moderation. You stay in control of what gets sent.

Insights for App Store Connect helps you:

- Chat across customer reviews, sentiment, complaints, and feature requests
- Ask Acquisition Chat questions across impressions, product page views, downloads, and conversion
- Generate review responses with context from the selected review
- Translate international reviews and preview translated replies
- Filter reviews by app version, territory, rating, and response status
- Track rating patterns, review volume, acquisition funnel metrics, and distribution
- Compare acquisition by source, territory, device type, and page type
- Prepare concise removal-request drafts for reviews that need escalation
- Keep App Store Connect review and acquisition work organized in a native Mac workspace

Insights for App Store Connect starts free, with optional Pro upgrades available in the app for more monthly intelligence credits.

App Store Connect credentials are stored securely in Keychain and used only to connect to App Store Connect. Insights for App Store Connect uses Apple-provided APIs but is not affiliated with or endorsed by Apple.

Manage your App Store reviews and acquisition from one focused Mac workspace.
